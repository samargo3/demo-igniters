- Standard requests: 1-2 business days
- Extended leave (5+ days): Up to 5 business days
- Emergency/sick leave: Notify manager immediately

### Article 7: Expense Report Submission Process

**Title:** How to Submit an Expense Report

**Article Type:** How-To

**Topics:** Finance, Expenses, Reimbursement

**Content:**

**When to Submit Expenses**
Submit within 30 days of purchase for reimbursement.

**Step-by-Step Process**

**1. Gather Documentation**
Required for each expense:
- ‚úÖ Original receipt (photo or scan)
- ‚úÖ Date of purchase
- ‚úÖ Vendor name
- ‚úÖ Amount paid
- ‚úÖ Business purpose

**2. Log into Expense System**
- Go to: expenses.company.com
- Use your company credentials
- Click "New Expense Report"

**3. Enter Expenses**
For each item:
- Upload receipt image
- Select category:
  - Travel (airfare, hotel, rental car)
  - Meals & Entertainment
  - Office Supplies
  - Client Entertainment
  - Professional Development
  - Other (explain)
- Enter amount
- Add business purpose/justification
- Tag project code (if applicable)

**4. Review and Submit**
- Double-check all amounts
- Ensure receipts are clear and complete
- Click "Submit for Approval"

**5. Track Status**
- Manager approval: 1-3 business days
- Finance review: 2-4 business days
- Reimbursement: 5-7 business days after approval
- Payment via direct deposit

**Expense Policies**

**Meals:**
- With clients: Up to $75/person
- Individual: Up to $50/day
- Alcohol: Only with clients, reasonable amounts

**Travel:**
- Flights: Economy class
- Hotels: Up to $200/night (major cities), $150/night (other)
- Rental cars: Mid-size or smaller
- Ground transportation: Reasonable (taxi, rideshare, public transit)

**Not Reimbursable:**
- ‚ùå Personal items
- ‚ùå Alcohol when dining alone
- ‚ùå Minibar charges
- ‚ùå Late fees or penalties
- ‚ùå Upgrades (flight, hotel, car)
- ‚ùå Personal entertainment

**Receipt Requirements:**
- Expenses over $25: Receipt required
- Expenses under $25: Receipt recommended
- Missing receipt: Complete "Lost Receipt Form"

**Tips for Faster Reimbursement:**
- Submit promptly (don't wait until month-end)
- Ensure receipts are legible
- Provide clear business justification
- Use correct expense categories
- Attach relevant documentation

Finance Team: expenses@company.com or ext. 6666
