## Contract 1: TechCorp International (Non-Compliant)

### MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Between:** Starburst Data, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And:** TechCorp International, Inc. ("Customer")  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Document Type:** Customer Paper MSA

#### 1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide data analytics platform services as described in attached Order Forms.

#### 2. PAYMENT TERMS
**Net 60 Days.** Customer shall pay all invoices within sixty (60) days of invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1% per month.

#### 3. ROLLOVER PROVISION
Unused committed capacity may rollover to the subsequent billing period up to a maximum of **fifteen percent (15%)** of the monthly commitment. Any rollover capacity not used in the following period shall expire.

#### 4. UPTIME SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Provider commits to maintain **99.95%** monthly uptime, calculated as the percentage of time the service is available excluding scheduled maintenance windows (not to exceed 4 hours per month).

**Service Credits:**
- 99.5% - 99.95%: 10% credit
- 99.0% - 99.5%: 25% credit
- Below 99.0%: 50% credit

#### 5. OVERAGE BILLING
Usage exceeding committed capacity shall be billed at **one point eight times (1.8x)** the committed rate, calculated on a monthly basis.

#### 6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
In no event shall Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement exceed **Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000)** per contract year, regardless of the number of claims.

#### 7. TERM AND TERMINATION
Initial term of twelve (12) months, automatically renewing for successive twelve-month periods unless either party provides ninety (90) days written notice of non-renewal.

**Risk Assessment:** HIGH  
**Non-Standard Terms:** Net 60 (should be Net 30), 15% rollover (should be ≤10%), 1.8x overage rate (should be 1.5x)

## Contract 2: DataFlow Systems (Compliant)

### MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Between:** Starburst Data, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And:** DataFlow Systems Ltd. ("Customer")  
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2025  
**Document Type:** Starburst Standard MSA

#### 1. SERVICES
Provider shall deliver enterprise data platform services per the specifications in the attached Order Form.

#### 2. PAYMENT TERMS
**Net 30 Days.** All invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month (18% annually).

#### 3. ROLLOVER PROVISION
Customer may rollover up to **eight percent (8%)** of unused committed capacity to the subsequent billing period. Rollover capacity must be consumed within that period or will expire without credit or refund.

#### 4. UPTIME SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Provider commits to **99.9%** monthly uptime, measured as the percentage of time the service is operational excluding scheduled maintenance (maximum 2 hours per month with 72-hour advance notice).

**Service Credits:**
- 99.0% - 99.9%: 15% credit
- 98.0% - 99.0%: 30% credit  
- Below 98.0%: 50% credit

#### 5. OVERAGE PRICING
Usage above committed capacity will be billed at **one and one-half times (1.5x)** the committed unit rate. This pricing structure encourages accurate capacity planning and commitments.

#### 6. BILLING TYPE
Customer shall be invoiced on a **Fixed Monthly Fee** basis as specified in the Order Form, regardless of actual usage up to the committed capacity.

#### 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Neither party's aggregate liability shall exceed the total fees paid or payable in the **twelve (12) months** preceding the claim, except for breaches of confidentiality, willful misconduct, or gross negligence.

#### 8. TERM
Initial term of twenty-four (24) months commencing on the Effective Date. Either party may terminate with thirty (30) days written notice after the initial term.

**Risk Assessment:** LOW  
**Compliance:** All terms meet Starburst standards

## Contract 3: CloudVision Inc (Critical Risk)

### MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Between:** Starburst Data, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And:** CloudVision Inc. ("Customer")  
**Effective Date:** February 10, 2025  
**Document Type:** Customer Paper MSA

#### 1. SERVICES
Provider will deliver real-time data analytics platform as specified in Exhibit A.

#### 2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment shall be made within **forty-five (45) days** of invoice receipt.

#### 3. ROLLOVER PROVISION
Unused capacity shall rollover at a rate of **twenty percent (20%)** per billing cycle, cumulative for up to three (3) billing cycles.

#### 4. UPTIME GUARANTEE
Provider guarantees **99.999% (five nines)** uptime on a monthly basis. Any failure to meet this threshold results in:
- 99.99% - 99.999%: 25% monthly credit
- 99.9% - 99.99%: 50% monthly credit
- Below 99.9%: Full month credit plus termination right

#### 5. BILLING MODEL
**Usage-Based Billing.** Customer shall pay only for actual queries processed, calculated monthly at the rates in the Order Form. Minimum monthly commitment of $10,000.

#### 6. OVERAGE TERMS
N/A - Usage-based billing model

#### 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's liability shall not exceed **the greater of** (a) Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000) or (b) the total fees paid in the preceding twelve (12) months.

#### 8. DATA OWNERSHIP
All data processed, including metadata and query patterns, remains the sole property of Customer.

**Risk Assessment:** CRITICAL  
**Non-Standard Terms:** Net 45, 20% rollover (excessive), 99.999% uptime (nearly impossible), usage-based billing (product limitation), high liability cap

## Contract 4: AnalyticsPro Ltd (Compliant)

### MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Between:** Starburst Data, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And:** AnalyticsPro Ltd. ("Customer")  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025  
**Document Type:** Starburst Standard MSA

#### 1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION
Provider shall furnish cloud-based data analytics platform services as detailed in attached Order Form #APL-2025-001.

#### 2. PAYMENT TERMS
**Net 30.** Customer agrees to remit payment within thirty (30) days of invoice date. Provider reserves the right to suspend services for payments overdue by more than fifteen (15) days.

#### 3. CAPACITY ROLLOVER
Customer may carry forward up to **five percent (5%)** of unused committed capacity to the immediately following billing period only. Rollover expires if not used within that period.

#### 4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Provider commits to **99.9% availability** per calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance (maximum 4 hours quarterly with 7-day notice).

**Remedies:**
- Below 99.9%: 10% service credit
- Below 99.5%: 25% service credit
- Below 99.0%: 50% service credit and termination right

#### 5. OVERAGE CHARGES
Consumption exceeding committed capacity incurs charges at **1.5 times** the standard rate, billed monthly in arrears.

#### 6. BILLING STRUCTURE
**Fixed Monthly Subscription** of $25,000 per month for committed capacity of 10TB data processing.

#### 7. LIABILITY CAP
Each party's total liability is limited to fees paid in the **twelve-month** period immediately preceding the claim, excluding indemnification obligations.

#### 8. CONTRACT TERM
Thirty-six (36) month initial term with automatic renewal for successive twelve-month periods unless terminated with sixty (60) days advance written notice.

**Risk Assessment:** LOW  
**Compliance:** 100% - Exemplary contract

## Contract 5: Enterprise Data Co (Medium Risk)

### MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Between:** Starburst Data, Inc. ("Provider")  
**And:** Enterprise Data Company ("Customer")  
**Effective Date:** May 15, 2025  
**Document Type:** Hybrid MSA (Customer paper with Starburst addendum)

#### 1. SERVICE PROVISION
Provider delivers enterprise data query and analytics services per the Service Order.

#### 2. PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS
Customer shall pay all invoices **Net 45 days** from invoice date. Overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.5% monthly.

#### 3. CAPACITY ROLLOVER POLICY
Up to **ten percent (10%)** of unused monthly committed capacity may rollover to the next billing period. Customer must use rolled capacity before current period capacity.

#### 4. AVAILABILITY COMMITMENT
Provider targets **99.9% uptime** measured monthly. Service credits apply for verified downtime exceeding this threshold.

#### 5. OVERAGE BILLING METHODOLOGY
Usage above commitment billed at **1.6x** committed rate, aggregated monthly.

#### 6. BILLING TYPE
**Fixed Monthly Fee:** $50,000 for 20TB committed capacity.

#### 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's aggregate liability capped at **Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($750,000)** annually or fees paid in prior twelve months, whichever is greater.

#### 8. AUDIT RIGHTS
Customer may audit Provider's usage records once annually with fourteen (14) days notice.

#### 9. TERM
Twenty-four (24) months initial term, auto-renewing for twelve (12) month periods with ninety (90) days termination notice.

**Risk Assessment:** MEDIUM  
**Non-Standard Terms:** Net 45 (should be Net 30), 1.6x overage rate (should be 1.5x)

## USAGE NOTES FOR DEMO

### High-Risk Contract (CloudVision):
Use this to demonstrate the agent flagging critical issues:
- 99.999% uptime is nearly impossible
- 20% rollover is excessive
- Usage-based billing has product limitations
- Shows how agent can save deal desk from accepting dangerous terms

### Compliant Contracts (DataFlow, AnalyticsPro):
Use these to show what "good" looks like:
- All terms meet standards
- Agent confirms compliance
- Low risk assessment
- These are the target state

### Inconsistency Example (TechCorp Order Form):
Use this to demonstrate document verification:
- MSA says 15% rollover
- Order Form says 10% rollover
- Agent catches the discrepancy
- Suggests which document controls

### Trend Analysis:
With all 5 contracts, agent can show:
- 60% have Net 30 (3 out of 5: DataFlow, AnalyticsPro, goal)
- 40% have ≤10% rollover (2 out of 5: DataFlow, AnalyticsPro)
- Average rollover: 13.6% (5% + 8% + 10% + 15% + 20%) / 5
- 80% use fixed monthly billing (4 out of 5; CloudVision is usage-based)
- 1 critical risk (CloudVision), 1 high risk (TechCorp), 1 medium (Enterprise Data), 2 low (DataFlow, AnalyticsPro)

## CREATING THE PDF CONTRACTS

### Method 1: Google Docs/Microsoft Word
1. Copy contract text above
2. Format with headers and proper spacing
3. Add company logos (optional for demo)
4. Export as PDF
5. Upload to Salesforce Files on appropriate Account/Opportunity

### Method 2: Quick Markdown to PDF
```bash
# Install pandoc if not already installed
brew install pandoc

# Convert markdown to PDF
pandoc contract-techcorp.md -o TechCorp-MSA.pdf
pandoc contract-dataflow.md -o DataFlow-MSA.pdf
# ... repeat for each contract
```

### Method 3: Use Template Tool
- Use the provided text in a contract template generator
- Many free tools available online
- Export as PDF

---

## UPLOADING TO SALESFORCE

1. Navigate to Account or Opportunity record
2. Go to **Files** tab
3. Click **Upload Files**
4. Select contract PDF
5. Add to both Account (for MSA/NDA) and Opportunity (for Order Forms)
6. Ensure files are visible and searchable
